# Knowledge Base - Articles for Vera

## Overview

This knowledge base contains reliable and authoritative resources for young American adults (18-30 years old) in the areas of personal finance, mental health, career development, and life skills. All resources come from government agencies, established non-profit organizations, and official sources.

## Core Resource Categories

### Personal Finance Resources

#### Government Financial Education Portals
- **MyMoney.gov** - Official U.S. government portal for financial education programs and information
- **Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB)** - Consumer protection and financial tools from consumerfinance.gov
- **Consumer.gov** - Reliable FTC information on money management basics and avoiding scams
- **Investor.gov (SEC)** - Official SEC investor education website with tools and information
- **FINRA Investor Education** - Unbiased information on money and investment issues from FINRA
- **FDIC Money Smart** - Free educational program providing practical knowledge and skills
- **Federal Student Aid (StudentAid.gov)** - Official U.S. Department of Education resource for college financing

#### Additional Financial Resources
- **nefe.org** - National Endowment for Financial Education
- **jumpstart.org** - Jump$tart Coalition for Personal Financial Literacy
- **HUD Helping Americans** - Housing resources from the Department of Housing and Urban Development
- **211.org** - Information and referrals for social services
- **USA.gov** - Official U.S. government information and services portal

### Mental Health & Mindfulness Resources

#### Government Mental Health Resources
- **MentalHealth.gov** - U.S. government's one-stop access to mental health information and resources
- **National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH)** - Lead federal agency for research on mental disorders
- **SAMHSA's National Helpline** - 1-800-662-HELP (24/7 free and confidential helpline)
- **988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline** - Nationwide three-digit number for crisis support

#### Crisis Support Services
- **Crisis Text Line** - Text HOME to 741741 for 24/7 mental health support
- **National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI)** - Nation's largest grassroots mental health organization
- **NIH's NCCIH** - Evidence-based information on meditation and mindfulness practices

#### Additional Mental Health Resources
- **Anxiety and Depression Association (ADAA)** - Resources for anxiety and depression
- **American Psychological Association (APA)** - APA help center resources
- **Mental Health America** - Mental health resources and support

### Career and Life Skills Resources

#### Government Career Resources
- **CareerOneStop (U.S. Dept of Labor)** - Comprehensive career development portal sponsored by DOL
- **USA.gov** - Official guide to U.S. government information and services with practical articles

#### In-House Content Topics
- **"Study Smarter, Not Harder"** - Time management and study tips for students
- **"Career Kickstart"** - Resume building, interview preparation, and networking for young professionals
- **"Smart Shopping and Budgeting Hacks"** - Shopping and budgeting advice for young adults
- **"Mindfulness and Stress-Relief Techniques"** - Mindfulness exercises for busy young adults
- **"Organizing Your Life"** - Productivity and organization tips for first apartment living

## Integration Notes

### For Knowledge Base Crawlers
- **Primary Sources**: Government websites (.gov domains)
- **Secondary Sources**: Established non-profit organizations
- **Content Types**: Educational materials, tools, guides, and directories
- **Update Frequency**: Monthly review recommended for accuracy

### Crawling Strategy Recommendations
- **Government Sites**: Use "recursive" crawling with max_depth=2
- **Resource Directories**: Use "single" crawling for specific pages
- **State Agencies**: Use "sitemap" crawling for comprehensive coverage
